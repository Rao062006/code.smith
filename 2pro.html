About Amity University

Established with a vision to provide quality education and foster research and innovation, Amity University has emerged as a leader in higher education.

World-Class Education Since 2005

Amity University is a leading private university in India with campuses across the country and international locations. We are recognized for our academic excellence, research contributions, and industry collaborations.

Our state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and innovative curriculum ensure that students receive education that prepares them for global challenges.

Amity has been consistently ranked among the top private universities in India by various ranking agencies and has collaborations with over 200 international universities.
