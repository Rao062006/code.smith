About Amity University Bangalore

Amity University Bangalore is a part of the Amity Education Group, India's leading education group with campuses across India and international locations. Established in 2010, the Bangalore campus spans over 60 acres of lush green landscape with state-of-the-art infrastructure.

Recognized by UGC and accredited by NAAC with 'A' Grade, Amity Bangalore offers over 100 programs across disciplines with a strong focus on research, innovation, and industry collaboration.

  • 60+ Acre High-Tech Campus
  • 300+ Industry Collaborations
  • 100+ Academic Programs
  • 500+ Faculty Members
